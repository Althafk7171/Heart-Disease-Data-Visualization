# Images

This folder contains visualization images generated during the Heart Disease Data Visualization project.

## Files

- `age_distribution.png` - Shows the distribution of patient ages in the dataset.
- `heart_disease_distribution.png` - Shows the distribution of patients with and without heart disease.
- `correlation_heatmap.png` - Shows correlations between numerical health-related features.
- `cholesterol_vs_heart_disease.png` - Compares cholesterol values based on heart disease status.
- `age_vs_maxhr_jointplot.png` - Shows the relationship between patient age and maximum heart rate achieved.
